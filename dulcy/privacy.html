Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

At Dulcy, we firmly believe in minimalist design not only in our interface but in the way we respect your personal information. Our main premise is simple: your data belongs to you, and we don't want it.

1. Zero Data Collection

The Dulcy app does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information or analytics to external servers. Your entire gear and lens library is stored strictly locally on your device, safeguarded by Apple's iOS ecosystem.

2. Cloud Sync (iCloud)

If you choose to store your collection using iCloud and the device's native infrastructure, that information gets backed up encrypted to Apple's own servers synced to your Apple ID. We, as developers of Dulcy, do not have any visual, telematics, or technical access to your library and we do not retain copies of your content.

3. Application Permissions

Dulcy does not request invasive or unnecessary operating system accesses. Any requested resource will be optional and purely functional for the app to operate.
